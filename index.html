Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Easy HVAC?

Easy HVAC is the easiest way to shop for and buy heating and cooling equipment with installation. Use the video camera on your mobile device to show us your home while having a conversation with us. We'll design your system, ship it to you, and arrange professional installation.z

What do I need to do?

Just download the free app and call us. We'll take care of the rest.

How long until I get a new system running in my house?

5 - 10 days on average. If you need it sooner, let us know, as faster options may be available.

What is the equipment warranty?

Manufacturer's warranties on parts are usually up to ten years. Details will be provided with quote. Labor warranty is one year. We provide free lifetime tech support.

What happens after I place an order?

We ship the products directly to you, delivered inside your home or garage. We'll call you to schedule an installation date that works for you, and the contractor of your choice will perform the installation.

How does the contractor get paid?

Once the installation is complete and you've indicated you're satisfied, we pay the contractor on your behalf.
